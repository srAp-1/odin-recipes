Lassi

Description

A chilled glass of lassi made with yogurt to cool down on a hot summer day. Prepare sweet or salty lassi at home with this easy recipe made with a handful of ingredients in just a few minutes.

Ingredients

  • 750 gram yogurt
  • 50 gram ice cubes
  • salt or sugar
  • 200 ml water

Steps

  1. Crush the ice cubes in a blender.
  2. Add the yogurt, water, salt or sugar
  3. Blend for about 1 minute.
  4. Serve chilled.
  5. If you have making salty lassi you can add specis like cummin and black salt
