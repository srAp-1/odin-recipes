Coconut Balls

Description

Heavenly white sweet coconut balls made with condensed milk and milk powder.

It's easy and delicious and a healthy option when you're having those sweet tooth carvings.This dessert recipe is an interstin dish to serve on occasions like parties and festivals.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of grated coconut, dessicated
  • ½ cup milk powder
  • 1 tin condensed milk
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • ½ tsp cardamom powder
  • A few tbps of dessicated coconut to garnish

Steps

  1. Heat ½ tbsp ghee in a heavy bottomed vessel.
  2. Add coconut and saute for 4-5 minutes on low flame.
  3. Add ½ cup milk powder and mix. Turn off heat and cool.
  4. Grease you hand, make small coconut ladoo balls and roll in dessicated coconut.
  5. Tips: In an air tight container, place the balls in your fridge for longer shelf life. At room tempertature they stay fresh up to 2-3 days.
